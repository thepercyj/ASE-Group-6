What is Polysphere Pro ?

Polysphere pro is a highly visual two-dimensional puzzle game that people of any age can enjoy. To play with it, users simply have to arrange a variety of interconnected spheres to duplicate structures or move and manipulate the spheres to create original designs!.


Start from level 1 with partial configuration if you are a beginner or go PRO by selecting level 5.
