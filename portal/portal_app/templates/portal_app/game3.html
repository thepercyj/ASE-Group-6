Polysphere Extreme

Gameplay

This game is a professional puzzle solver where players manipulate and solve puzzles with pyramid-shaped structures and three-dimensional spherical objects. Challenges may involve adjusting or placing the different pieces within a three-dimensional space to get a pyramid structure.

Background

As a professional puzzle solver, players in this puzzle needs to have creative thinking. Each steps to solve this puzzle has different kinds of tricks and developer of this puzzle solver are intended to introduce the another professional level of puzzle game to those who would like to improve their creative thinking skills.

How many solutions ?

There are 26,720 possible solutions by using Donald Knuth algorithm X in this puzzle solver.

Limitations

Placement of the shapes can only be done unidirectional relative to the initial placement of the first sphere. Placing the pieces diagonally is limited as there are some limits in skewing the shapes of pieces from their original shape. The limitation is caused due to heavy reliance on libraries which are used to generate the 3D element of the puzzle.
