What is Polysphere Extreme ?

Polysphere Extreme is a highly visual three-dimensional puzzle pyramid game that people of any age can enjoy. To play with it, users simply have to arrange a variety of interconnected spheres to duplicate structures into a pyramid or move and manipulate the spheres to create original designs!.
