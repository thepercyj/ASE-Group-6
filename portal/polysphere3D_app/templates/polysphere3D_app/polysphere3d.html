How to Play

    Instructions

  • Select a puzzle and try clicking the relevant colors as shown in the image inside the 3D canvas.
  • Arrange the provided spheres on the pyramid board to match a chosen puzzle or create your unique structure.
  • Spheres must be placed on the board in such a way it does not overlap each other.
  • Ensure each sphere fits within the designated grid spheres.
  • Experiment with different sphere arrangements to design your own structures.
  • Connect spheres in creative ways to fill the board.
  • Successfully complete a puzzle by filling the entire pyramid with the provided spheres.
  • Confirm that your arrangement matches the structure shown in the puzzle solutions.

    • Tips for Success

  • Pay attention to sphere shapes and connection points.
  • Think strategically to optimize space and complete the puzzle efficiently.
  • Polysphere Extreme is not just a game but also a tool for spatial reasoning and creative thinking.
  • Have fun exploring different designs and improving your puzzle-solving skills.
