What is Kanoodle ?

Kanoodle is a highly visual three-dimensional puzzle game that people of any age can enjoy. To play with Kanoodle, users simply have to arrange a variety of interconnected spheres to duplicate structures in their Kanoodle book. Or, move and manipulate the spheres to create original designs! Choose a Starting Point: Start with an empty board or an array to represent the board. The board is typically a 5x11 grid, and you'll place Kanoodle on it.
